My history

Let's go!

Hello, my name is Emanuele, I'm 15 years old, I'm student and I'm from Brazil. I'm christian and this blog is about my life, especially my life with my hobbies and my Faith! I enjoy doing handcrafthings, waching k-dramas, eating new foods and visiting new places. I love take pictures and it allows me to expand the treasures of the world.

Ok, I want start with my conversion! All start in the pandemic, where my family and I spend a lot of the time in the internet. I was already a Christian, but my relation with Jesus was horrible. I almost never read the bible, much less, prayied. And when I prayied, the moments were ordinary without much meaning.

But, in the year 2021, in november, I start join a new community, not that a new church would bring me back to the way. So, in 2022, in march, I think, I did my Encounter with Gog, for my new church. And it went beyond just another moment of prayer or worship, but it was a watershed in my life. I had a true spiritual experience.

And until then, I'm in the best time in my life, with a new reality, new conviviality. Not is easy, but the award is amazing. BECAUSE WITH HE, I HAVE THE AMAZING GRACE!
